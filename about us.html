NYAFTISANS

Our Mission

To provide artists a digital space where they can showcase their crafts and earn fair commissions from their work, while connecting them to a wider audience that values creativity, and authenticity.

Our Vision

To become a trusted platform that uplifts artists by transforming creativity into sustainable success, inspiring a global community where handmade craftsmanship is valued, supported, and celebrated.

Our Values

To uphold creativity by empowering artists to express their individuality, integrity by ensuring fairness, transparency and ethical practices in every interaction, and community by fostering an inclusive space for all, that encourage collaboration, inspiration, and shared growth.
